White Sauce Lasagna

  • Large oven-safe rectangular casserole dish
  • One yellow onion (large)
  • Cooking oil of choice (olive)
  • Couple cloves of garlic
  • 1.5lb of ground beef (any %, we use lean)
  • 2 ~14.5 oz cans of petit cut tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1.5 tbsp of chopped dried basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 2.5 tbsp of flour
  • 4 cups of mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Aluminum foil
  • (Optional) Ketchup or two tbsp of tomato paste
  • (Optional) Parmesan, shredded or grated

Meat Sauce

  1. Chop onion and garlic very finely.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion until soft in large pot, using cooking oil of choice on high heat.
  3. Add in ground beef, stirring until cooked thoroughly.
  4. Add ~2 cans of petit cut tomatoes.
  5. Add ~1.5 tbsp chopped dried basil leaves, add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Cover and simmer on medium-low heat ~20-25 minutes until some (not all) of the liquid has boiled off.
  7. (Optional) Add two tbsp of tomato paste (or squeeze of ketchup) to thicken.

White Cheese Sauce

  1. Melt ~2 tbsp of butter in medium pot on low-medium heat.
  2. Add in ~2.5 tbsp of flour and stir thoroughly.
  3. Adding in a small pour of milk at a time, on low-medium heat, stir vigorously to incorporate the milk into the butter-flour mixture. Do this one small pour at a time until all 3 cups of milk are incorporated.
  4. ‼️ Take your time! Make sure that the milk that is poured into the pot a little bit at a time is well-incorporated before next small pour of milk, to prevent unwanted clumping. It should become easier to incorporate the milk into the butter-flour mixture after a few rounds.
  5. Once all the milk is used, bring the pot to boiling point, then turn the heat down as to not boil over, then simmer for ~3 minutes.
  6. Add ~2.5 cups of mozzarella and stir.
  7. Once the cheese is incorporated, add salt to taste.
  8. Keep on low and stir, as to not let the sauce film over if you are still working on boiling the lasagna strips.

Lasagna

  1. Boil large pot of water.
  2. Add salt and olive oil to taste.
  3. Boil 9-12 pieces of lasagna strips. Follow directions on the box.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven for 400 °F
  2. Carefully add ~1/3 of cheese sauce on bottom of the dish, using a ladle.
  3. Add one layer of lasagna strips over the cheese sauce.
  4. Add half of the meat sauce over the lasagna strips.
  5. Add one layer of lasagna strips over the meat sauce.
  6. Add the rest of the meat sauce over the lasagna strips.
  7. Add one layer of lasagna strips over the meat sauce.
  8. Ladle in rest of sauce over the meat sauce, making sure the whole dish is covered.
  9. Spread ~1.5 cups of mozzarella on top evenly.
  10. Sprinkle parmesan on top.< /li>
  11. Cover with foil.
  12. Cook for ~20-25 minutes at 400 °F.
  13. Take off foil and cook for ~10 minutes or until slightly browned.
